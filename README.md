# SUPERSTORE-SALES-AND-PROFIT-ANALYSIS-DASHBOARD-Power-Bi-Project
This Power BI project presents a comprehensive analysis of Superstore sales data through interactive dashboards. The dashboard helps track important KPIs such as Total Sales, Total Profit, Profit Margin, and Total Orders while providing insights into regional performance, customer behavior, product contribution, and sales trends.
📊 Project Overview

This Power BI dashboard analyzes Superstore sales data to provide insights into sales, profit, customers, and products. It helps businesses monitor performance, identify trends, and make data-driven decisions through interactive visualizations and KPIs.


---

🎯 Dashboard Features

✔️ Executive Overview with key KPIs (Sales, Profit, Orders, Margin)

✔️ Sales Analysis by Region, Category, Segment, and Time

✔️ Profit Analysis across Products and Regions

✔️ Customer Analytics with customer contribution and purchase patterns

✔️ Product Analytics showing top-performing products and discount impact

✔️ Interactive slicers and drill-down capabilities


---

🛠️ Tools Used

🔹 Power BI Desktop

🔹 Power Query (Data Cleaning & Transformation)

🔹 DAX (Data Analysis Expressions)

🔹 Superstore Dataset

🔹 Data Modeling & Relationships

🔹 Interactive Visualizations and KPI Cards


---

💡 Key Business Insights

📈 Total Sales reached 2.30M

💰 Total Profit achieved 286K

🌍 West Region generated the highest sales and profit

👥 Consumer Segment contributed the largest share of revenue

🏆 Canon products were the top performers in sales and profit

📉 Higher discounts negatively impacted profitability

📊 California emerged as the best-performing state

🚚 Standard Class was the most preferred shipping mode
